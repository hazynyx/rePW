# PWEnhancer v3.0.0 🚀

**PWEnhancer** is the ultimate Physics Wallah lecture optimization suite. Built from the ground up to save you time and maximize your study efficiency. 

Featuring a natively integrated Jumpcutter engine, perfectly isolated Time Saved analytics, and real-time TrueTime prediction—all wrapped in a stunning, modern UI.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### ✂️ Integrated Jumpcutter Engine
Automatically detect and skip silence during your lectures.
- **Auto-Silence Skipping:** Blasts through pauses and silent board-writing at high speed (e.g. 4.5x) while slowing back down to your base speed (e.g. 2.0x) the instant the teacher speaks.
- **Soft-Disable Toggles:** Turn Jumpcutter on and off seamlessly without ever needing to reload the video page.
- **Advanced Tuning:** Fully customize Volume Threshold, Margin Before, and Margin After from the extension popup.

### ⏱️ TrueTime Predictions & Isolated Analytics
Know exactly how long your lecture will *actually* take.
- **TrueTime Pill:** A beautiful, non-intrusive floating pill on the video player that calculates your **True Time Left** based on your current custom speed.
- **Isolated Math Engine:** Accurately isolates and splits the exact physical time you save between "Custom Speed" savings and "Jumpcutter" savings, preventing any double-counting.
- **Daily History Dashboard:** A full analytics dashboard (`analysis.html`) featuring a daily heatmap, Study Calendar, and isolated Time Saved buckets.

### 📸 High-Res Slide Capture
Capture clean, DRM-bypassed screenshots of your lecture slides.
- **One-Click Capture:** Instantly save high-resolution slides without any player UI in the way.
- **Lightbox Expansion:** Click to zoom and expand timeline slides natively in the browser.

### 🎨 Sleek Dark Mode & Zen Features
- **Cinematic Dark Mode:** Inverts harsh white themes into a smooth, battery-saving dark mode.
- **Auto-Hide Cursor:** Focus on the lecture without distractions.

## 🛠️ Installation

1. Download the latest release from the [Releases page](https://github.com/0xLittleDream/PWEnhancer/releases).
2. Unzip the downloaded folder to a safe location.
3. Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
4. Enable **Developer mode** in the top right corner.
5. Click **Load unpacked** and select the unzipped `PWEnhancer` folder.
6. Pin the extension and start studying!

---

## ⚙️ How it Works

- **To toggle Jumpcutter:** Use the `>>` button directly on the TrueTime video pill, or toggle it from the extension popup.
- **To adjust Native Speed:** Use the `+` and `-` buttons on the TrueTime pill to adjust your baseline speed.
- **To view Analytics:** Click the **Open Analysis Dashboard** button inside the extension popup.
